Accessibility Statement

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Cylio LLC ("Cylio") is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We continually work to improve the accessibility and usability of our digital presence.

Our Commitment

We strive to ensure that our website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as a guiding standard. We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are continuously working to improve our website's accessibility.

Current Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following features to support accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML markup for improved screen reader compatibility
  • Alternative text for informational images
  • Keyboard-navigable interactive elements
  • Sufficient color contrast for text and important visual elements
  • Responsive design for various devices and screen sizes
  • Clear and consistent navigation structure

Known Limitations

While we strive for full compliance, some content on our website may not yet fully meet all WCAG 2.1 Level AA criteria. We are actively working to identify and address any gaps. Specific known limitations may include:

  • Some older media content may lack complete alternative text descriptions
  • Certain third-party embedded content may not be fully accessible

Feedback and Assistance

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website, or if you need information in an alternative format, please contact us. We welcome your feedback and will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your needs:

  • Email: support@cylio.io
  • Phone: +1 (718) 781-7927

Please include a description of the specific content or feature you found inaccessible, and we will work to address it.

Third-Party Content

Our website may include content or features provided by third parties. While we encourage our partners and vendors to provide accessible content, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content or external websites linked from our site.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvements and regularly review our website to identify and resolve accessibility issues. This statement will be updated as we make progress.
