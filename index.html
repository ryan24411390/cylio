What is the web design process like at Cylio?

As a leading digital agency, we've honed our web design process into a streamlined six-step journey that ensures collaboration and creativity at every stage. Our goal is to create bespoke, high-performing websites that not only look beautiful but also drive results for your business.

  1. Discovery and Strategy: We kick off by getting to know your business, audience, and goals. No one understands your brand better than you, so we’ll tap into your insights and data, including tools like Google Analytics, to build a strong foundation for the project.
  2. Concepting Phase: In the web concepting phase, we take everything we’ve learned in phase one and bring your vision to life. Our team presents carefully crafted design concepts that reflect your brand’s goals and user needs, providing multiple creative directions for the path forward. This stage is all about collaboration—we’ll refine these concepts based on your feedback to ensure the final design aligns perfectly with your business objectives and stands out in the marketplace.
  3. Design Phase: Our team then crafts the rest of the website based on the approved concept chosen in phase 2. In this phase of web design, we also prioritize user experience (UX) and intuitive UI design to ensure a seamless and engaging visitor journey.
  4. Development and Implementation: Once you approve the design, our developers build your website from the ground up, ensuring that every detail is pixel-perfect and tailored to your needs.
  5. Testing: Before launching, we thoroughly test the site across all devices and browsers to ensure optimal performance and usability.
  6. Launch and Support: After a successful launch, we offer ongoing support to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and optimized for growth.

Our collaborative approach ensures your website is not only a reflection of your brand but also a tool for business growth. Let’s create a digital experience that drives success!

We're having trouble with our website, can you fix it?

At Cylio, we no longer take on projects that involve fixing websites developed by another agency. While we understand the challenges businesses face with underperforming websites, our goal is to deliver high-quality results that meet our clients’ expectations. To achieve this, we prefer to handle the entire process—from strategy and design through to development—ensuring we provide a cohesive and optimized website experience.

Attempting to fix a website built by another agency often requires extensive discovery and analysis by our web design team to understand its structure, third-party integrations, and overall functionality. This process can be just as time-consuming as creating a new, custom website design from scratch, with limited improvements to show for the effort. In most cases, the cost of repairing a site approaches that of building a new one, which is why we believe in offering a fresh start to ensure your site performs and looks its best.

By focusing on delivering comprehensive custom website design solutions, we ensure that every aspect of your site is tailored to your needs, built for optimal performance, and aligned with your business goals. This commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as a leading website design agency.
